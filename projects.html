Semiconductor Device Fabrication

Brown EE · Jan–May 2025

Designed pn-junction diode devices in KLayout (GDSII layout editor) and carried out full fabrication in the cleanroom: maskless direct-write lithography (MLA-150), wet etching (BOE), furnace annealing at 1000°C, metal deposition via e-beam evaporation (PVD), and lift-off.

Performed electrical characterization via I-V measurements to calculate the average ideality factor. Applied Statistical Process Control to determine an effective device yield of 75%.
